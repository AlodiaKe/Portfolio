11TP1: EGG DROP

This was our very first Product Design assessment of the year. We were told to build a contraption that would stop an egg from cracking after being dropped from a great height. Except there was one catch - The only material we could use was a sheet of A3 card, tape, and hot glue. The image to the left is a concept/design I made for my project. It includes a parachute and accordion legs to absorb the impact of the fall. In the end, my egg ended up surviving the fall!

11TP1: UMBRELLA STAND

This is our final Product Design assessment, and I am currently working on it. Over the course of 2 terms, we had to design, create mockups, and build a piece of flatpack furniture. This was a tough challenge, as all pieces had to fit in a box with the dimensions 420 x 300 x 145 (mm). I am creating an umbrella stand, and the coolest thing about it is that zero screws are required. The image to the left is a model I made in Fusion360, with the stand in it's environment (my deck). I am very proud of my work so far, and am predicting I will get an Excellence (If I can actually finish in time).
